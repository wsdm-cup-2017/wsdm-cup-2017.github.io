Wikidata Vandalism Detection

Given a Wikidata revision, is it damaging?

This task is about detecting vandalism as well as all other kinds of damaging edits to Wikidata. A vandalism detector for Wikidata protects its integrity much the same as they do on Wikipedia, only more so: protecting Wikidata's integrity also protects that of all information systems making use of Wikidata.

Wikidata Entity Search

Given type-like Wikidata triples, compute the degree to which the subject belongs to the type.

This task tackles a fundamental problem of search in knowledge bases, namely to decide which triples of a subject are more relevant to it compared to others. The task will address a select set of type-like relations, including "occupation" and "country of citizenship".
